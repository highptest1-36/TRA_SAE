                                 run_phase2_grpo.py (and logic/physics variants)
  - Evaluation scripts        : experiments/step0_canonical_eval.py,
                                 step1_rerun_cfg0_3.py, step2_multiseed_cfg3.py,
                                 step8_fair_baselines.py, step9_external_benchmark.py

                                 step7_stats_extra.py, step12_error_reannotation.py
